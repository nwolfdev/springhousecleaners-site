**Cleaning Tips for an Egg-cellent Easter**

With Easter just around the corner, now is the perfect time to give your home a little TLC. Here are a few egg-ceptional cleaning tips to get you started:

- **Dust, Dust, Dust:** Before you start decorating for Easter, give your home a good dusting. Don't forget to pay special attention to those often overlooked areas like ceiling fans, baseboards, and behind furniture.

- **Say Goodbye to Stains:** Hosting an Easter egg dyeing extravaganza? Keep your surfaces stain-free by wiping up spills and splatters as soon as they happen. A little vinegar and water can work wonders on stubborn stains!

- **Freshen Up Your Fabrics:** Give your linens and upholstery a refresh by throwing them in the wash or giving them a good steam. Nothing says "welcome spring" like clean, fluffy towels and crisp, wrinkle-free curtains!

- **Don't Forget the Floors:** Whether you have carpet, hardwood, or tile, make sure to give your floors some love this Easter. Vacuum carpets, mop hardwoods, and sweep away any crumbs or debris that may have accumulated.
