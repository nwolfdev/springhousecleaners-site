**1. Prepare Ahead of Time:** One of the best ways to ensure your bathroom stays tidy during a gathering is to prepare it in advance. Here's a checklist to help you get started:

- **Deep Clean:** Start by giving your bathroom a thorough cleaning a day or two before your guests arrive. Pay attention to the sink, toilet, and bathtub or shower. Our team at Spring Cleaning can help with [deep cleaning](https://springhousecleaners.com/deep-cleaning-service/) tasks to ensure everything sparkles.

- **Stock Up:** Make sure you have an ample supply of essentials like toilet paper, hand soap, and clean towels. You don't want your guests to run out or struggle to find these items when they need them. Remember [2020](https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/coronavirus-toilet-paper-shortage-panic/2020/04/07/1fd30e92-75b5-11ea-87da-77a8136c1a6d_story.html)?

- **Declutter:** Remove any personal items or clutter from your bathroom countertops and shelves. This not only makes the space feel more open but also prevents your guests from accidentally knocking things over.

- **Air Fresheners:** Consider using air fresheners or essential oil diffusers to keep the bathroom smelling pleasant throughout the event.

**2. Set Up a Guest Basket:** To make your guests feel even more welcome and ensure a tidy bathroom experience, create a guest basket. Here's what you can include:

- **[Disposable Hand Towels](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=paper+guest+towels+for+bathroom&hvadid=557452807137&hvdev=c&hvlocphy=9027726&hvnetw=g&hvqmt=e&hvrand=6868900224425721337&hvtargid=kwd-13531373128&hydadcr=15309_13496737&tag=googhydr-20&ref=pd_sl_2jbgygv4jb_e):** Place a stack of disposable hand towels on the countertop or in a decorative basket. This encourages guests to use these towels instead of your regular ones, keeping your bathroom neater.

- **[Toiletries](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=travel+size+toiletries&crid=192FLFLJUQ2BC&sprefix=travel+sized+t%2Caps%2C159&ref=nb_sb_ss_ab-sx-reranking-v3-desktop_1_14):** Provide basic toiletries such as travel-sized shampoo, conditioner, and body wash. This is especially thoughtful if your guests are staying overnight.

- **Trash Can:** Ensure there's a small trash can with a liner in an easily accessible spot for disposing of used towels or other disposable items.

**3. Enlist Help During the Event:** Maintaining a tidy bathroom can be challenging when you're busy hosting. Consider designating someone, whether it's a family member or a close friend, to periodically check and tidy up the bathroom during the event. Their duties can include:

- **Emptying the Trash:** Make sure the trash is emptied regularly to prevent it from overflowing.

- **Restocking Supplies:** Ensure that hand soap, disposable hand towels, and toilet paper are replenished as needed.

- **Wiping Surfaces:** A quick wipe down of countertops and sink can work wonders in keeping the bathroom looking clean.
