### Tips for Maintaining a Clean Workspace Between Professional Visits

While we’re here to handle the heavy lifting, maintaining a clean workspace between our visits is essential. Here are a few simple tips:

- **Encourage Desk Cleanliness**: Encourage employees to keep their desks organized and free from clutter.

- **Implement a Clean-as-You-Go Policy**: Encourage staff to clean up after themselves, whether it’s in the breakroom or meeting spaces.

- **Provide Adequate Trash and Recycling Bins**: Make sure bins are easily accessible to encourage proper disposal of waste.

- **Disinfect High-Touch Areas**: Regularly wipe down high-touch areas like doorknobs, keyboards, and phones to reduce the spread of germs.
