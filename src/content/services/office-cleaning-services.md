## A workspace that reflects how you actually operate.

The state of your office isn't just about appearances. It's about how your team feels when they walk in on Monday morning, how clients experience your space in the first thirty seconds, and how often people get sick during cold and flu season.

We clean offices across the Houston area — from small professional suites to multi-floor operations — with the same care and consistency we bring to our residential clients.

## What we handle

Every office is different, so every scope is different. The most common items we cover:

- **Workstations and common areas:** desks wiped and disinfected, chairs dusted, keyboards and phones sanitized
- **Conference rooms:** tables, chairs, AV remote and whiteboard surfaces
- **Kitchens and break rooms:** counters, appliances (inside and out, as needed), sinks, floors
- **Restrooms:** full sanitization of toilets, sinks, floors, dispensers; restocked as agreed
- **Floors:** vacuumed, mopped, spot-cleaned; tile grout detailed on requested cadence
- **High-touch areas:** door handles, light switches, elevator buttons, shared tech
- **Trash:** emptied, liners replaced, recycling handled per your sorting setup

## Scheduling that works around you

Most of our commercial clients prefer after-hours or early-morning service so we're out of the way during work hours. Nightly, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — we build the cadence that makes sense for your traffic and your budget.

We also offer one-time commercial deep cleans for post-construction cleanup, seasonal resets, or after a team has moved into a new space.

## Green by default

We use plant-based, non-toxic products by default — safer for your team, safer for anyone with allergies, and better for indoor air quality. If you need hospital-grade disinfectants for specific areas (medical, dental, food service), we can accommodate that too.

## A single point of contact

You'll have one person to call, text, or email — not a customer service line. If something needs to change or an issue comes up, you get a real response from someone who actually knows your account.
