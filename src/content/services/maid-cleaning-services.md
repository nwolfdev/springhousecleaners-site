## A consistent clean, on your schedule.

Most of our customers don't want to think about cleaning. They want to walk into a home that's already clean, and stay that way. That's what recurring maid service is for.

You pick the cadence — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. We show up on the same day each time, with (whenever possible) the same cleaner who already knows your space. No starting from scratch on every visit. No re-explaining the dog's water bowl situation.

## What a typical visit looks like

We bring everything — products, equipment, the whole setup. You don't need to have a thing ready. A typical recurring visit covers:

- Dusting all surfaces, fixtures, and furniture
- Vacuuming and mopping all floors
- Full bathroom service: toilets, showers, tubs, mirrors, counters
- Full kitchen service: counters, sink, stovetop, exterior appliances
- Making beds, tidying visible clutter
- Taking out trash and replacing liners
- Wiping high-touch areas — doorknobs, light switches, remotes

## Cadence options

**Weekly.** For busy households, families with young kids, or anyone who wants to live in a consistently clean home without lifting a finger.

**Bi-weekly.** The most popular option. Catches up before it builds up. Strikes the balance between cost and quality of life for most homes.

**Monthly.** Great for smaller spaces, empty-nesters, or homes that stay pretty tidy between visits but want a deeper pass once a month.

## Getting started

Most first-time customers book an initial deep clean (to get everything to baseline) and then move into recurring service from there. But it's not required. If your home is already in good shape, we can start with a standard first visit.

Eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products are available on request — just let us know when booking if you have kids, pets, allergies, or chemical sensitivities, and we'll bring the right supplies.

Either way, we'll walk through your space, ask about priorities, and send you a clear quote before you commit to anything.
