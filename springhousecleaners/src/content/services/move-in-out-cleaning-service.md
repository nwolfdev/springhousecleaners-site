## Spotless handoff, whether you're arriving or leaving.

Moving is already one of the most stressful things you can do. The last thing you need is to be on your hands and knees scrubbing a previous tenant's shower at 10pm before the lease handover.

That's what move-in/out service is for. You focus on boxes, logistics, and whatever fresh chaos has decided to show up that day. We handle getting the space truly clean — the kind of clean that holds up to a landlord's final walkthrough, or welcomes you into a new home that actually feels like yours.

## What move-out cleaning covers

The goal is simple: get the space to a condition that passes any inspection and maximizes your deposit return. That means:

- **Every surface** wiped down and sanitized — inside cabinets, drawers, closets, pantries
- **Appliances** cleaned inside and out: oven, microwave, fridge (moved and cleaned behind, if accessible), dishwasher, range hood
- **Bathrooms** deep-cleaned, including grout, shower tracks, behind toilets, exhaust fans
- **Floors** vacuumed, mopped, and edge-detailed; carpet spot-treated where needed
- **Fixtures** polished: faucets, handles, light switches, outlet covers
- **Baseboards, door frames, windowsills, and vents** all wiped

If your lease has specific requirements (carpet cleaning, wall washing, etc.), we'll build them into the scope up front.

## What move-in cleaning covers

Starting fresh in a new place deserves a fresh start. Move-in service covers the same deep detail as move-out, with extra attention to the things you don't want to discover three weeks in:

- Inside every cabinet, drawer, and closet
- Full appliance sanitization
- Deep bathroom clean — grout, fixtures, every inch of tile
- Floors detailed in every corner, including inside closets and pantries
- Air vents, light fixtures, and ceiling fan blades wiped
- High-touch surfaces sanitized throughout

You walk into a home that smells clean, feels clean, and is ready for life to start.

## Timing and pricing

Move cleans are priced by home size and condition. Most 2-3 bedroom homes take a two-person team four to six hours. We can almost always accommodate short-notice bookings — tell us what you need and when, and we'll make it work.

Free quote, and a clear scope in writing before we start.
