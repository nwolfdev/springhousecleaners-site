### Crafting Your Cleaning Calendar

**Assess Your Needs:** Take a moment to assess your home's cleaning needs. Identify high-traffic areas, prioritize tasks, and note any specific areas that require extra attention.

**Set Realistic Goals:** Be realistic about what you can accomplish. Break tasks into manageable chunks and spread them across the weeks or months, keeping in mind your schedule and energy levels.

**[Establish a Routine](https://www.marthastewart.com/home-cleaning-schedule-checklists-7377969):** Whether it's a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedule, consistency is key. Assign specific tasks to particular days or weekends to create a rhythm that works for you.
